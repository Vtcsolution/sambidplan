## The 10 AI Tools — What Each Does (Real Examples)

### 1. AI Summarize
**Input:** You click "Summarize" on any contract
**Behind the scenes:** System loads all 40+ fields from SAM.gov (dates, contacts, office chain, award data, performance period)
**Output:**
> 8(a) set-aside for IT services. NAICS 541512. Navy SPAWAR, San Diego.
> Value: $340K. Due: July 15, 2026 (22 days left).
> POC: John Smith, john.smith@navy.mil, (619) 553-2001.
> Performance: Sep 2026 – Sep 2028 at Naval Base San Diego.
> RED FLAG: 22-day response window is tight. Start immediately.

### 2. AI Bid Analysis
**Input:** You click "Bid Analysis"
**Behind the scenes:** Fetches 25 similar awards from USASpending + your company profile (UEI, certs, past wins)
**Output:**

> 23 similar contracts in NAICS 541512 awarded in last 3 years.
> Top competitors: AURORA SYSTEMS (5 wins, $1.2M), TECHNICA CORP (3 wins, $890K).
> Your company: 2 wins ($180K) — relevant past performance.
> This is 8(a) set-aside — your certification qualifies.
> **Suggested bid: $310K–$380K** (market average: $340K).

### 3. AI Competitive Intelligence
**Input:** You click "Competitive Analysis"
**Behind the scenes:** Pulls real winners from USASpending, compares against your verified profile
**Output:**

> 2. TECHNICA CORP — 3 contracts, $890K, strong Navy relationship
> 3. CYBER SOLUTIONS — 2 contracts, $340K, similar size to you
>
> **Your position:** 2 wins ($180K). You're competitive against #3.
> **Strategy:** Price at $320K (undercut AURORA's average of $380K).
> **Weakness:** No Navy past performance. Consider teaming with Navy-experienced sub.

- Management Plan (addresses actual performance location and timeline)
- Past Performance (your REAL USASpending wins + positioning against REAL competitors)
